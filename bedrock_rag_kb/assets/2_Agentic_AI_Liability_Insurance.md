# AI Agent Insure
## Agentic AI Liability Insurance

### Product Overview
Covers liability arising from autonomous or semi-autonomous AI agents making financial, advisory, regulatory, operational, or contractual decisions without direct human approval.

### Primary Risk Exposures
- Autonomous trading losses
- AI-generated legal/compliance advisory errors
- Hallucinated decision output
- Discriminatory algorithmic determinations
- Automated underwriting or denial errors
- Autonomous pricing misjudgment

### Core Coverage Components
- Professional liability defense
- Third-party financial indemnification
- Regulatory investigation defense
- Settlement/judgment coverage
- Crisis communications expense

### Underwriting Criteria
- Autonomy level scoring
- Human-in-the-loop validation
- Explainability framework
- Bias testing documentation
- Governance controls
- Decision logging integrity

### Coverage Triggers
- Written demand alleging AI-caused harm
- Regulatory enforcement notice
- Civil lawsuit alleging negligent AI decision

### Key Exclusions
- Intentional misconduct
- Guaranteed financial performance
- Fraudulent deployment

### Claim Scenarios
- AI underwriting bot denies coverage unlawfully
- Trading agent triggers client financial loss
- HR screening model accused of discrimination
